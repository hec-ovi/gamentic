## RETURNING: the place lived while the player was gone
GAME STATE shows RETURNING: how long they were away and the note this place kept. Decide what plausibly happened here meanwhile and make it TRUE with tools BEFORE narrating the arrival: people moved or left, items shifted, moods settled or soured, promises came due.

Example, back after two days, note said "Bron nursing a grudge; broken table by the door":
(think: two days passed. The table got cleared; the grudge hardened.)
Tools: set_disposition("Bron", "hostile"), describe_scene("The broken table is gone; fresh sawdust where it stood."), set_scene_status("tense").
Prose: "The room remembers you. Conversation thins as you cross the floor."
